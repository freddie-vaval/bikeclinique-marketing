# 🎬 FILMING CHEAT SHEET
## The Bike Mechanic's Masterclass — What to Film, In Order

---

## BEFORE YOU START

**Set up once:**
- Phone on tripod, eye level with component
- Lapel mic on, test audio
- Ring light or workshop LED on
- Repair stand in frame
- Background: workshop, clean, bikes visible

**Check every take:**
- Audio is clean (can hear tool sounds clearly)
- Component is in focus
- Good lighting on the part you're working on

---

## FILMING ORDER

### DAY 1: MODULE 1 (Foundation — Easy)
*Freddie warming up, talking to camera*

1. **Lesson 1:** Walk around your workshop. Talk about setting up a workspace. Film the repair stand, the torque wrench, the tool wall.
2. **Lesson 2:** All tools laid out on mat. Pick each one up, explain what it does and why you bought it.
3. **Lesson 3:** Bike on stand. Point at each part, explain anatomy. No editing needed — just talk.
4. **Lesson 4:** Serious talk to camera about safety and standards. This one film once, cleanly.
5. **Lesson 5:** Pick up a random bike. Do a 60-second assessment out loud, thinking process visible.

---

### DAY 2: MODULE 2 (Maintenance — Core Skills)
*Have bikes ready in dirty and clean states*

6. **Lesson 6:** Dirty bike being cleaned. Degreaser, brush, rinse. Real work.
7. **Lesson 7:** Tyre off, puncture repair. Have a thorn puncture ready. Show the tube, show the repair, show the reinflation.
8. **Lesson 8:** Chain on chain tool. Wear gauge. Show worn vs new. Clean and lube.
9. **Lesson 9:** Gears being indexed. Barrel adjuster turned while pedalling. Derailleur limit screws shown.
10. **Lesson 10:** Brake pads replaced on rim brakes. Centring adjusted.

---

### DAY 3: MODULE 3 (Intermediate — Most Valuable Content)
*Have bikes in for servicing — real jobs*

11. **Lesson 11:** Full hydraulic brake bleed. Close-up on lever, syringe, bleed process.
12. **Lesson 12:** Wheel on truing stand. Show tensioning, show true being pulled in.
13. **Lesson 13:** Bottom bracket removal and installation. Have 2-3 different types to show.
14. **Lesson 14:** Headset strip and service. Fork removed, bearings cleaned, repacked, reassembled.
15. **Lesson 15:** Dropper post service. Air sleeve or full service if you have one apart.
16. **Lesson 16:** Full inspection on a customer's bike. Do the walk-around, narrate what you're seeing.

---

### DAY 4: MODULE 4 (The Build — The Hero Content)
*The big production day. Film in one session if possible.*

**Have all components laid out before camera starts.**
**Consider a timelapse of the full build with Freddie voiceover.**

17. **Lesson 17:** Stem installation. Spacers, star nut, stem on, torque wrench used.
18. **Lesson 18:** Crankset and bottom bracket. Full install, torque sequence.
19. **Lesson 19:** Electronic shifting. Battery, derailleur adjustment, Synco chip app shown.
20. **Lesson 20:** Build Part 1. Frame to rolling chassis. Frame arrives, BB in, headset in, fork in, stem on, wheels on.
21. **Lesson 21:** Build Part 2. Rear derailleur, chain sizing and install, brakes bled from scratch, cable routing.
22. **Lesson 22:** Build Part 3. Final torque check, test ride.
23. **Lesson 23:** The spec consultation. Freddie on camera explaining how he specs a bike for a customer. Use a real or imagined customer scenario.

---

### DAY 5: MODULE 5 + MASTER LESSONS (Business — Personality + Premium)
*Freddie relaxed, talking directly to camera. This day films the business content.*

**Module 5:**
24. **Lesson 24:** Mobile vs workshop. Freddie's story and model.
25. **Lesson 25:** Pricing. Calculator on screen. Real numbers explained.
26. **Lesson 26:** Getting customers. What works, what doesn't.
27. **Lesson 27:** Insurance and legal. Serious talk, no jokes here.
28. **Lesson 28:** Van tour. Walk around the van, show the setup.
29. **Lesson 29:** Diagnostics. Creaking bike, Freddie diagnosing live, thinking out loud.
30. **Lesson 30:** What's next. Looking at camera, motivational close.

**Master Tier Exclusives (same day, film after Lesson 30):**
31. **Master 1 — Boss Mindset:** No tools, just Freddie to camera. Deep, personal, opinionated. This is the most important lesson in the entire course. No script — just Freddie talking from the heart about the business side.
32. **Master 2 — Profit Blueprint:** Screen-share with calculator. Real numbers, real margins. Show the Profit Calculator. This is filmed, not talked — show the actual spreadsheet.
33. **Master 3 — Scalable Mechanic:** Freddie + case studies. Talk through how to hire, how to systemise, how to grow.
34. **Master 4 — Personal Brand:** Freddie on camera about his own experience building BikeClinique's reputation. What worked, what didn't.

## EDITING NOTES

**Minimum editing:** Cut the starts and ends of takes. No need to over-edit mechanic content — authenticity is the brand.

**Music:** Subtle background music during lessons — royalty-free from YouTube audio library.

**Text overlays:** Use CapCut. Add key terms in bold text when a technical word is introduced. "This is the DERAILLEUR — it moves the chain between gears."

**Thumbnail formula:** Your face + a tool + a bike = the thumbnail. Make them consistent.

**Course trailer:** Clips from your best moments. 2 minutes. Hook: "I learned to build bikes the hard way. You don't have to."
