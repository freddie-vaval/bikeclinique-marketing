## Template 1: Guest Post Pitch (Publications)

**Subject:** [Article idea] — can we contribute to [publication]?

**Body:**
```

I run the workshop at BikeClinique, a specialist custom bike build and servicing shop in Clapham, South London.

I came across [publication] and noticed you cover [topic area]. I recently wrote a guide on [specific topic] — [title] — that I think your readers would find genuinely useful.

Here's the outline:
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]
- [Point 3]

I'd love to contribute this as a guest post. I've attached a draft intro if helpful, or I can send the full piece once we agree on the angle.

The article would be original to [publication] and I'd include a brief bio pointing to BikeClinique.

Happy to discuss further.

Fred
BikeClinique — Custom builds and specialist servicing in South London
```

## Template 5: Reddit Engagement (Not a cold email — for when answering questions)

**When responding to a Reddit post where someone asks about:**
- "How much should bike servicing cost?"
- "Is [shop] any good?"
- "Can anyone recommend a bike mechanic in [area]?"

**Format:**
```
[Your helpful answer — 3-5 sentences, genuinely useful]

[Optional: I run [shop] — if you're in [area] happy to take a look. No obligation.]
```

**Rules:**
1. Answer the question first, always
2. Do not mention your shop unless it's directly relevant
3. Link to your blog post if you have one that directly answers the question
4. Never spam, never cold-drop links

## Outreach Tracking Notes

Track every outreach in: `marketing/link-building-outreach-tracker.csv`

For each target:
- Date sent
- Method (email / LinkedIn / Reddit / form)
- Response status (no response / interested / published)
- Follow-up date (5 days after initial)

**Key rule:** Personalise every email. Reference something specific about their publication. Mass generic emails get ignored.
